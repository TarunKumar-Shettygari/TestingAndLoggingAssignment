Stapler Unit Test Cases:

1.Load stapler with full pins.
2.Load stapler with few pins.
3.Load stapler with zero pins.
4.Load stapler with pins more than the limit.
5.Load stapler with smaller size pins.
6.Load stapler with larger size pins.
7.Check working of spring in all the above cases.
8.Stapple few papers.
9.Stapple many papers.
10.Stapple papers more than the limit.
11.Stapple without pins.
12.Stapple thick papers or cardboard.
